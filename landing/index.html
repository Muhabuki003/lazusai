Questions

Frequently asked

Everything owners ask us before going live.

Does it work with my existing phone number?
Yes. If your business number can receive iMessage, we connect it directly. If not, we provision a dedicated number for you during onboarding — your customers just save one number and text it like normal.
What happens when the AI can’t answer something?
It escalates instead of guessing. The conversation is flagged, you get an instant alert with the full context, and a human takes over. The customer is told someone from the team will follow up — nothing is left hanging.
Can I jump into a conversation myself?
Any time. Every conversation happens on your number, so you can reply from your own phone or the dashboard and the AI steps back. Your customers see one continuous thread.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live the same day — often within the hour. You give us your services, pricing, hours, and common questions; we configure the AI, run test conversations with you, and hand over the dashboard.
What about voice messages?
Handled automatically. When a customer sends a voice note, it’s transcribed and the AI responds to what was said, just like a text.
Am I locked into a contract?
No. Every plan is month-to-month and you can cancel whenever you like. Your conversation history and leads are always exportable.
